About Me

Writer | Director | Casting Dir | Editor

Sabrina Z. Geffner is a passionate NYC based writer/director currently working in narrative short forms. Sabrina loves to show relatable, universal human struggles through the lens of unique surreal dramas, fantasy, and comedic stories. She has experience in production assistant work, production design, editing, and casting directing. She also strives to create diversity in the film and TV entertainment industry for young Asian women like herself. Sabrina is working towards her BFA in Film Directing at the School of Visual Arts.
